About Us

A home that started with just five children

It started in 2012 with just 5 children. With persistent effort and hard work, our team is able to provide not only shelter, food and education to underprivileged children, tribal kids and child labourers — but also a carved-out home where these blossoming buds are given enough love and care to feel emotionally, physically and economically secure.

To meet the demand of the place, the team goes door-to-door and collects donations. We are trying to carve out a disciplined lifestyle in children. Kindly visit the Ashrama to see the brightness in these children.

Our Vision

A society where the old aged live in comfort and dignity in the last phase of life, and children get love, care and guidance which makes each of them a good human being — empowering distressed women and working to build a healthy, hunger-free world.

🎯

Our Mission

To make our little kids stand on their own and become good citizens for our nation.

What We Do

Building hope, creating lasting change

Our core programmes reach children, women and communities — building a healthier, more dignified future for everyone we serve.

🏠

Hostel for Children

A safe, nurturing home with food, shelter and a disciplined, caring environment for underprivileged children.

📚

Education

Primary, middle and high schooling for children, plus assistance for girls to pursue higher education.

👩‍🍳

Women Empowerment

Income-generating activities, entrepreneur development, awareness and home-products training for women.

🩺

Medical Camps

Health camps and medical assistance, including support for the physically challenged.

🌱

Environment & Farming

Organic farming awareness and environmental programmes for a greener, healthier future.

🤝

Community & Relief

Rural development, self-help groups, legal aid, flood relief and support during natural calamities.

Events Conducted

Moments from our journey

A look back at the celebrations, trips, awareness drives and community programmes our children and volunteers have been part of.

🎉 Cultural Celebration

Kannada Rajyotsava

Our children celebrated Karnataka's formation day with pride — carrying flags, offering prayers and honouring the culture and spirit of the state together as one family.

📍 Nagasandra, Bengaluru
🚌 Annual Outing

Yearly One-Day Trip

Every year the children enjoy a special day out — exploring historic forts and temples, taking in new sights, and making joyful memories beyond the Ashrama.

📍 Chitradurga & surroundings
📢 Awareness Drive

Child Adoption Awareness Programme

Together with student volunteers, we spread awareness about legal child adoption and the 1098 child helpline — reaching out to the public through posters and street outreach.

📍 Community outreach, Bengaluru
🎗️ Health Awareness

World AIDS Awareness Programme

Volunteers and students came together for a World AIDS Day drive — raising awareness through posters, a human-chain formation and public engagement across the neighbourhood.

📍 Nagasandra & nearby areas
🔬 Learning & Innovation

Science Exhibition

Our children showcased their creativity and curiosity at a science innovation exhibition — building working models on themes like renewable energy, human evolution and saving the earth.

📍 School science fair
🧘 Health & Wellness

Yoga Day

The children came together for a yoga session — practising postures, balance and breathing to build strength, discipline and calm as part of their daily wellbeing.

📍 SSSACT grounds, Bengaluru
🎨 Creativity

Art & Drawing Activity

A day of creativity and inspiration — the children visited an art space and proudly displayed their own drawings, nurturing imagination and self-expression.

📍 Art museum visit, Bengaluru
